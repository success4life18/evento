Discription

Imperial Event Hall?is an indigenous event centre that specialises in the provision of world class event management and hospitality services. Located in a peaceful and well-secured environment opposite Regency Hall behind Abiola Gardens, Imperial Event Hall can take a banquet seating of 400, theatre seating of 500 and conference seating of 450 people. This event centre in Ikeja is equipped with tables, chairs, restrooms, a changing room, power supply, parking space, air conditioner, stage, security and a standby backup generator.
